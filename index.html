Project 1

TRAIN ACCIDENT AVOIDANCE

A train accident avoidance system using piezo sensors involves detecting pressure changes on railway tracks, particularly in areas prone to rockfalls or debris. Piezoelectric sensors are placed along the tracks to sense the force exerted by falling rocks or other obstructions. When unusual pressure is detected, the system triggers an alert to notify railway staff and can potentially stop an approaching train to prevent collisions and accidents, enhancing safety in hilly or hazardous regions.
